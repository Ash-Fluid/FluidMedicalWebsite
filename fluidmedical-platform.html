Precision
01

Lab-grade urinalysis.
At home.

Precision turns your phone camera into a research-grade urinalysis reader. Hold it over one of our patented test strips and get results in seconds — the same markers a lab would measure, in the privacy of your own bathroom.

With two primary tiers under development covering 30+ urine markers in total, Precision rapidly scales from already available daily wellness and nutrition check-in to a comprehensive hormonal panel in under two years, while simultaneously developing further pipeline covering Metabolic, Gut Health, Mental Health, Longevity, and Detox areas of well-being.

  • 95–100% accuracy across all existing biomarkers validated by independent 3rd party lab
  • 7 patents protecting the strip and scan technology
  • Results stored privately — never shared without consent
  • Works offline — no cloud dependency for scanning

Daily Signal
02

Your wearable data,
in context.

DailySignal syncs heart rate, HRV, sleep architecture, steps, and resting energy from Apple Health, Samsung Health, Oura Ring, WHOOP, and Fitbit — and then interprets each reading against your strip results and personal context.

  • Apple Health, Samsung Health, Oura, WHOOP, Fitbit
  • Trends shown against your personal baseline, not global averages
  • Correlated with strip and Me+ data for full context
  • On-device processing — raw biometric data never leaves your phone

MyRecords
04

Your whole health history,
connected.

Upload your urine marker results, prescriptions, imaging reports, and clinical notes. Oria reads and indexes them — then visualizes connections across your entire uploaded history. Search anything. Share longitudinal data securely with any provider.

  • AI document parsing — PDFs, images, HL7, FHIR formats
  • Cross-referenced with strip and wearable history
  • Secure, time-limited sharing links for practitioners
  • Sovereign storage — fully under your control

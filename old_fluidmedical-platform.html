Results are stored in your secure data store, tracked over time, and interpreted alongside your wearable data and personal context. Not just a number — a pattern.

  • 8 biomarkers: Glucose, protein, pH, ketones, nitrites, leukocytes, specific gravity, blood
  • 95–100% validated accuracy against clinical lab benchmarks
  • Longitudinal trending — spot changes before symptoms appear
  • Secure share with your care team via one-time link
  • Cycle-aware interpretation — context matters

DailySignal

Your wearable data deserves context. DailySignal syncs heart rate, HRV, sleep stages, activity, and resting energy from your favourite devices — and interprets it all together, not in silos.

When your HRV drops and your urinalysis shows elevated leukocytes, Wave notices. That kind of connection is only possible when all your data lives in one place.

  • Live integrations: Apple Health, Samsung Health, Oura, WHOOP, Strava, Nuralogix
  • HRV trend analysis with cycle-phase context
  • Cross-signal pattern detection — connecting dots across data sources
  • Nuralogix face-scan: blood pressure, stress, and haemoglobin (Wave tier)

03 · Live Now

Fluid+Me

The context that makes everything make sense. Fluid+Me captures your cycle, symptoms, mood, medications, sleep quality, and lifestyle — the personal factors that shape how you feel and what your numbers mean.

Your Fluid+Me data never leaves your secure store without your explicit permission. It lives in your encrypted data store, not on a shared platform.

  • Menstrual cycle tracking with phase-aware AI insights
  • Symptom journalling — headaches, fatigue, pain, mood shifts
  • Medication and supplement logging
  • Nutrition and hydration markers
  • Your data, isolated in your own encrypted store — zero cross-contamination

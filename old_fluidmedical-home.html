The Wave Platform

Four features. One complete picture.

Most health apps track one thing. Wave connects your at-home lab results, wearable data, personal context, and medical records — so every insight is actually yours.

01
Fluid Precision

At-home urinalysis using our patented test strips. Scan with your phone camera. Get lab-grade results in seconds — glucose, protein, pH, ketones, nitrites, and more.

Live Now
02
DailySignal

Your wearable data — heart rate, HRV, sleep, steps, resting energy — synced from Apple Health, Samsung Health, Oura, WHOOP, and more. Interpreted in context, not in isolation.

Live Now
03
Fluid+Me

Your personal health context. Cycle tracking, symptoms, medications, mood, and lifestyle — the things that make your data make sense. Private. Yours. Never shared without permission.

Live Now
04
My Records

Upload your lab results, prescriptions, imaging reports, and clinical notes. Wave reads them with AI and connects the dots across your whole health picture. Search anything. Share securely.

Security & Data

Your health data
belongs to you.

Most health platforms monetise your most sensitive information. Wave was built differently — on infrastructure designed for industries where data sovereignty is non-negotiable.

The Problem

Most platforms treat you as the product.

Your health data is among the most sensitive information that exists. It tells insurers about your risk profile, tells advertisers about your vulnerabilities, and once it's out, you can't get it back. The industry's default is to harvest it, aggregate it, and sell it.

Data sold to advertisers

Many consumer health apps share anonymised (and sometimes not-so-anonymised) data with ad networks. Your symptoms. Your cycle. Your diagnoses. Sold to sell you things.

Shared with insurers

Health data shared with or sold to insurance data brokers can affect your coverage, premiums, or eligibility. Without your knowledge or consent.

No real control

Terms of service are designed to obscure what's actually happening to your data. You click "agree" and hope for the best. That's not consent. That's resignation.

Sovereign Data

One pod. Yours alone.

Every Wave user has their own isolated data pod. Your health data doesn't live in a shared database alongside thousands of other users. It lives in a dedicated, encrypted store that belongs to you — and only you can authorise access to it.

Zero cross-contamination. Your data cannot leak into another user's pod. There is no shared analytics layer, no pooled anonymisation, no aggregate data sale. Each pod is structurally isolated — not just policy-isolated.

Four Pillars

How we protect your data.

These aren't promises. They're architectural decisions.

1
Isolation

Your data pod is physically separated from every other user's data. Not just access-controlled — architecturally isolated. There is no route for your data to reach another user's pod, and vice versa.

2
Consent-first

Every share requires your explicit, specific consent. You choose who sees what, and for how long. Consent can be revoked at any time, instantly. No blanket permissions. No hidden sharing.

3
Portability

Your data is yours to take. Export your entire health record at any time, in standard formats. If you leave Wave, your data leaves with you — complete, unaltered, and immediately useful.

4
Transparency

We publish a clear, plain-language privacy policy. We tell you exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it. No legalese. No buried opt-outs. We don't sell data. Full stop.

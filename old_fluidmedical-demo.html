Four screens. One complete picture.

Navigate using the bottom tab bar in the prototype above.

Tab 01
Dashboard

Your real-time health overview — biomarker trends, wearable metrics (HRV, sleep, SpO₂), and daily movement. Everything at a glance, personalised to you.

Try tapping on individual metric cards to see trend detail.
Tab 02
My Records

Upload any health document — lab results, clinical notes, imaging reports — and watch the AI extract and index the key data automatically via Fluid's OCR engine.

Try uploading a document to see the AI extraction flow in action.
Tab 03
Fluid Precision

Your Fluid Precision at-home urine test results — 8–12 biomarkers from a 90-second test. View current results, historical trends, and what each marker means for you.

Results are colour-coded: green = normal range, amber = worth watching.
Tab 04
AI Health Query

Ask your health data anything — "How has my hydration changed this month?", "What does my creatinine trend mean?". The AI pulls from your actual data to answer.

Try one of the suggested query chips, or type your own question.

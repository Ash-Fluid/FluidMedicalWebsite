How it works

Four features. One complete picture.

Each feature is useful on its own. Together, they give you a level of health intelligence that no single-signal app can match.

01
Fluid Precision

Scan a strip. Get lab-grade results.

Use a Fluid Precision test strip with your morning sample. Hold it in front of your phone camera for 30 seconds. Oria reads the colorimetric result across up to 20 markers simultaneously — no separate reader hardware required. Results are available in the app within 60 seconds of scanning.

The first two foundational tiers are included in every Oria plan. Women's Health and Men's Health panels unlock with an add-on strip subscription.

Live Now
02
DailySignal

Wearable data, interpreted in context.

Connect Apple Health, Oura, WHOOP, Garmin, or any HealthKit-compatible device. DailySignal aggregates your heart rate variability, resting heart rate, sleep stages, activity, and stress scores — and cross-references them with your strip results and personal context.

Seeing elevated inflammation markers on a strip the day after a low-HRV night means something different than seeing them after a hard workout. DailySignal provides the context to tell the difference.

Live Now
03
Fluid+Me

Your personal health context — private, always.

Log your cycle, symptoms, medications, mood, and lifestyle factors. Fluid+Me is the contextual layer that makes everything else interpretable. A high leukocyte count looks different if you logged a UTI symptom earlier that morning. Elevated LH looks different on day 12 of a 28-day cycle than on day 20.

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it. Fluid+Me is end-to-end encrypted with your own key.

Live Now
04
My Records

Your clinical history, finally connected.

Upload lab results, imaging reports, prescriptions, and clinical notes. My Records uses Oria AI to read and structure them — extracting key values, flagging relevant history, and connecting dots across your complete health picture. A TSH result from two years ago becomes relevant context when your thyroid markers shift today.

All AI-generated insights are clearly labelled: insights, not medical advice.
